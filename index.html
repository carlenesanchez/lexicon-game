the lexicon project

the lexicon project is a gamified way to retain new words and actually use them in real writing & conversation, rather than just recognizing them on a flashcard.

how it works
  • add a word and we auto-pull its definition from the dictionary
  • flashcards test recall — flip to reveal the definition. correct = +10 xp
  • use it tests production — write your own sentence, then self-grade. correct = +15 xp, since actively using a word is worth more than just recognizing it
  • miss a word and it resets to the "new" box so it resurfaces sooner; get it right and it levels up (new → learning → familiar → mastered) with a longer gap before its next review
  • practice at least one word a day to keep your streak going
  • ★ star a word from your bank to practice it anytime — no need to wait for it to come due
